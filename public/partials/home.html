Current Incidents

This is only a basic DEMO application to simulate inciweb.nwcg.gov. InciWeb is an interagency all-risk incident information management system.

Use the search bar to filter one or many full terms (i.e. Wildfire Active). Filter phrases by surrounding in quotes (i.e. "Placerville Complex"). Exclude full terms by applying a preceding negation operator (i.e. -Wildfire) Use the icons to the right to add, edit, or remove the select incident.
